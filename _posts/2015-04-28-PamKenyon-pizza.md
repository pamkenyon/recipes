## Ingredients
- Pizza dough
- Italian red sauce
- Mozarella cheese
- Gouda cheese
- Cheddar cheese
- Onions

## Directions

Toss the dough into a round shape.  Don't drop it.  Spread sauce.  Sprinkle cheese.  Bake.  

pull back the cooked cheese layer.  splatter in some franks redhot sauce.  lay cheese layer back down.

Eat.
